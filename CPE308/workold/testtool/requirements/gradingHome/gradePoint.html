2.5.7 Grading Point-and-Click Question

This is the professor's home screen when trying to grade a Point-and-Click question.

  • Auto Grading: Theres is no auto-grading option available at this time.
  • Manual Grading: After evaulating the question and the student's answer(s) which is represented by the colored sphere, the professor can leave a note for the student and edit the point-value field by clicking on the grey box to input a score.
