2.3.3.1 Delete Questions


To delete a Question from the Test, check the check box next to the Question in Step 3. You can check multiple Questions to be deleted.
Then click the "DELETE" button on the right side of the pane.

For example, Figure 2.3.3.1.1 shows a partially completed test after Step 2. If the Instructor Decides that she does not want Questions 9 and 10, she can DELETE them using the method stated above.

Figure 2.3.3.1.1: Partially Completed Test Generated after Step 2


Figure 2.3.3.1.2 is the resultant Test after Deleting Questions 9 and 10

Figure 2.3.3.1.2: Partially Completed Test with 2 Questions removed

REMEMBER:


It is imperative for the Instructor to keep in mind, that DELETING a Question will effect the Total Time of a test.

The Instructor may wish to use the "SEARCH and REPLACE" function in the Edit pane to search for a replacement Question to keep the Total Time within the Total Time Range Constraint!
