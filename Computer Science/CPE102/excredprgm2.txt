JFrame:

add(Component)- adds something to the frame


Layout Managers:
-BorderLayout
--Default manager forJframe's content pane
--stuff you put on it grow to fit availiable space

-FlowLayout
--Default manager for JPanel
--stuff you put on it maintain PREFERRED size

-GridLayout
--Divides availiable space intto equally sized "cells" in specified row/columns
--stuff you put in a cell grows to fit cell
